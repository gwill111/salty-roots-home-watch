About Salty Roots

Salty Roots Home Watch LLC is a husband-and-wife owned and operated company founded by a retired U.S. Veteran and 7th generation Floridian. Over two decades of dedicated service to our country prepared us to now serve our local community with premium home watch services throughout Southwest Florida.

We understand the unique needs of Florida property owners — whether you are a snowbird, frequent traveler, or full-time resident. As a Certified Home Watch Academy Graduate, we follow professional standards. Our commitment is backed by full licensing and insurance.
