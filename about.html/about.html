About Salty Roots Home Watch LLC

Salty Roots Home Watch LLC is a husband-and-wife owned and operated company founded by a retired U.S. Veteran and 7th generation Floridian. Over two decades of dedicated service to our country prepared us to now serve our local community with premium home watch services throughout Southwest Florida.

We understand the unique needs of Florida property owners — whether you are a snowbird, frequent traveler, or full-time resident who wants extra peace of mind. As a Certified Home Watch Academy Graduate, we follow professional standards and best practices in the industry. Our commitment to excellence is backed by full licensing and insurance, so you can trust that your home is in capable, caring hands.

At Salty Roots, we combine modern technology with old-fashioned personal service. Every visit includes a detailed interior and exterior observation, timestamped photos, and a comprehensive report delivered straight to your inbox. Our mission is simple: deliver premium service rooted in strong values, honesty, and genuine care.

Premium Service — Rooted Values.
It’s more than our tagline — it’s our promise.
